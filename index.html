About Us

At Envenco, we believe in excellent service at an affordable price. We strive to provide customer service that is highly professional, punctual, affordable, reliable, and with safety in mind. Our services are offered to a variety of clients, including investors, property owners, property managers, industrial facilities, real estate developers, construction contractors, governmental agencies, public and private sectors clients.

Envenco's team includes Professional Engineers (P. Eng.) and Professional Geoscientists (P. Geo.), serving Rwanda and International organizations.

Envenco offers to our customers nearly 15 years of diverse technical and engineering experience in the areas of civil engineering, environmental, and building science. Our core services are typically for government, industrial, commercial, multi-unit apartment buildings, and institutional facilities.
